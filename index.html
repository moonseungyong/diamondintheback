Diamond in the Back
Information
A YouTube upload, a simple error, and suddenly, Curtis Mayfield had a hit he never recorded: "Diamond in the Back." This strange case of mistaken musical identity reveals a fascinating truth: we can easily accept something as real, even when it isn't. This website explores that phenomenon in the age of AI. Just as "Diamond in the Back" took on a life of its own, the AI here conjures images and content that feel strikingly authentic, yet are entirely synthetic. Is it "ours"? Is it "theirs"? Diamond in the Back is a space for us to ponder these questions, to examine the increasingly blurry line between human creation and artificial generation.
